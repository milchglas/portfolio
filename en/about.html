about me.


Hi! I'm Niclas, the person behind artofbokeh and a photographer based in the beautiful city of Wernigerode, Germany.

I picked up a camera 4 years ago and since then I was obsessed with photography. When people ask what kind of photographer I am, I usually answer with, "Well I shoot a little bit of everything", which is very true. You name it: weddings, landscapes, candids, pets, flowers... the list goes on... My goal is to create not only fantastic looking photos but also create a fun and memorable experience. When I'm not with my camera, I study media computer science at the Hochschule Harz. Besides that I play lots of Volleyball and go on hikes with friends.
